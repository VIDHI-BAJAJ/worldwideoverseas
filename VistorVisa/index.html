Vistor Visa

A visitor visa, also referred to as a tourist visa, grants temporary entry to individuals wishing to travel to another country for leisure, tourism, or to visit family and friends. Typically, these visas have limited validity periods and prohibit the visa holder from engaging in employment or business activities during their stay. Requirements for obtaining a visitor visa vary by country but often include demonstrating financial capability, intent to return home after the visit, and proving no intention to overstay or engage in unauthorized activities. It's crucial to thoroughly research and adhere to the specific visa regulations and application procedures of the destination country.
